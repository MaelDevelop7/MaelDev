About Me

I'm Mael, a web developer with a strong passion for building interactive digital experiences. I specialize in crafting intuitive and responsive interfaces using modern front-end technologies like TypeScript, React, and Tailwind CSS.

My focus is on creating applications that not only look good but also deliver great user experience. I value clean code, performance, and maintainability in every project I work on.

I’m also highly interested in tech ecosystems like web-based operating systems, productivity tools, and custom UI environments. One of my most ambitious projects is CloudOS, a virtual desktop environment built entirely in the browser.

I’m constantly learning and exploring new tools and frameworks to stay at the forefront of front-end development. Whether working on personal projects or collaborating with a team, I bring curiosity, creativity, and consistency to the table.
